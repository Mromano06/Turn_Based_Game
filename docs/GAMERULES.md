# Game Rules
There are 2 players per game, and eventually an AI will be added so that single player is possible. 
The grid is rectangular and varies in size depending on the playersâ€™ choice, and eventually I will implement random grids with obstacles. 
For now, stats are going to be as basic as possible, health, energy/ap and damage. 
Attacking and moving cost energy, and each turn some energy is regained. Distance that a player can move is determined by their energy, 1 energy equals 1 space you can move. 
Attacks work a little differently, a player can only attack adjacent squares, each attack also costs energy. For now, Things will stay as simple as they can get.
